Oats Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Oats
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 30 grams pistachios
  • 20 grams honey
  • milk
  • blueberries
  • apple (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the base: In a jar or bowl, add 1 cup of oats. Pour in enough milk to fully cover the oats (about 1–1.5 cups, depending on how thick you want it).
  2. Mix in protein: Stir in 1 scoop of protein powder until fully dissolved with the oats and milk. This will enrich the oats with flavor and nutrition.
  3. Add sweetness: Drizzle 20 grams of honey into the mixture and stir well so the sweetness is evenly spread.
  4. Incorporate nuts: Roughly chop or crush 30 grams of pistachios and mix them into the oats for crunch and healthy fats.
  5. Refrigerate overnight: Cover the jar or bowl and refrigerate for at least 6–8 hours (or overnight) so the oats absorb the liquid and soften.
  6. Add fresh toppings: In the morning, stir the oats and top with fresh blueberries. If desired, dice an apple and add it for extra crunch and fiber.
  7. Serve chilled: Enjoy straight from the fridge, or add a splash of extra milk if you prefer a looser consistency.
